About Us

Eat & Enjoy

Just Eat is a fully online based food order delivery system. It was founded in 2022. During the Covid-19 pandemic, people was unable to go to the restaurants and enjoy their food. So we focused on bringing the food to you. We are dedicated to creating wonderful surprises that you'll absolutely love. We are dedicated to deliver your food in time and provide a good quality food.
